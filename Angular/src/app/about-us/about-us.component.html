What is TJH?

A web application that allows users to SHARE , READ AND REACT stories based on different categories that are available.

Categories currently available:
  • FUNNY
  • INSPIRING
  • ANGRY
  • EMOTIONAL

GOALS :

  • Allow users to read stories according to their choice and mood.
  • Decrease unnecessary scrolling to find good content.
  • Less energy consuming, more fun.
  • View stories even without being logged in
  • Upload and react on the stories as you feel ! (login feature )
